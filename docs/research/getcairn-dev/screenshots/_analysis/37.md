## Summary
A right-side detail panel displays the causal position of a selected node, identified as a "System capstone." It enumerates upstream enablers (USV Platform, Power Generation, ROV Vehicle, Launch & Recovery, Communications & Data Link, Autonomy, Control & Mission Payload), and presents a four-tile dependency summary (Descendants 6, Requirements 9, Interfaces 2, Gaps Below 6). A pull quote from Harney's Technology Evaluation, Ch. 1 reinforces the capstone metaphor, and two action buttons let the user refocus the pyramid on this node or analyze causality. A lower right "Delete" affordance appears at the foot of the panel.
