## Summary
This screen shows a system-level detail page within the Causality tab, focused on an "Offshore Survey USV-ROV" entity. The header displays the SYSTEM badge, title, and a wrapped description introducing a hybrid diesel-electric Unmanned Surface Vessel tethered to a Remotely Operated Vehicle controlled from a shore-based Remote Operations Centre. Below sits a centred SYSTEM card and a DOMAIN TECHNOLOGIES grouping that lays out four SUBSYSTEM cards (USV Platform, Power Generation & Distribution, ROV Vehicle, Launch & Recovery System) plus a Communications & Data Link card and an Autonomy, Control & Mission Payload card. A PARTS & MATERIALS row begins to render at the bottom showing four GAP placeholder tiles in muted red.
