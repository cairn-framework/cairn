## Summary
This screen shows a "Units" tab inside what appears to be a typed primitives or measurement registry, sibling to a "Data Types" tab. A six-row table catalogues units with their ID, name, symbol, dimension, kind classification, and SI conversion factor. The kind column distinguishes "si" base units (in teal) from "derived" units (in orange), and the SI factor column gives the multiplier to convert into SI base. A "+ Add Unit" affordance sits in the top right.
