## Novel observations
- Introduces a dedicated "Quality Check" surface that aggregates findings into typed buckets (errors / warnings / info) with a single headline count, a portable pattern for any spec linter UI.
- Findings are scoped by subsystem identifiers using a "SUB-<token>" prefix (SUB-USV, SUB-PWR, SUB-ROV, SUB-COMMS, SUB-AUTO), suggesting a structured node-id taxonomy distinct from artifact ids.
- "COMPLETENESS" and "TRACEABILITY" appear as parallel sibling categories, hinting at a multi-dimensional quality model (this view focuses on completeness; traceability is a peer section).
- The remediation language pairs two distinct corrective paths in one hint: "Add requirements" (declare intent at this node) or "decompose this node into children" (push the obligation downstream). This is a transferable phrasing for spec-linter remediations.
- The "Entire Model" vs "This Node" toggle introduces a global/local scope switch dedicated to lint findings rather than to navigation.
- Re-run plus timestamp suggests the check is a discrete job with a last-run wall-clock marker rather than a live evaluation.
