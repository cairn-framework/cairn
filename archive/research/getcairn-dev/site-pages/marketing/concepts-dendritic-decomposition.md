## Deep-Sea Survey AUV

### REVEALED

Begin with the Mission

Identify the immutable constraints. What cannot be engineered around.

Before any engineering: what must this vehicle do? Survey the ocean floor at 3,000m depth for 72 continuous hours, autonomously. These three numbers (depth, time, autonomy) are the seed from which the entire design grows. Every decision downstream traces back here.
